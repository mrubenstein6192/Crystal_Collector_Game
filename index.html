Crystal Collector

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific number of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random target score. You lose the game if your total score goes over the target score.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.
  • Each time the game starts, the values of the crystals will change.
